About DjSoft

Startup founded by M.Sc. Eng. Jimmy Sánchez: Trilingual Systems Engineer (Spanish, English and Portuguese) of the National University of Colombia with a Master's Degree in Systems Engineering from the Universidad de los Andes. Experience of more than 13 years under different roles in software development projects (mainly web applications) using different technologies such as Visual Studio .NET, ASP.NET MVC, C #, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX and Microsoft SQL Server; and 2 years as director of advanced research projects in information technologies. With knowledge in good practices such as PMBOK, PSP, TSP and Scrum. In search of job opportunities in development, architecture and technical leadership of projects of software, coaching of personnel specialized in systems engineering, research, development and innovation (R+D+i). With leadership ability, creativity, organization, problem solving, innovation and analytical thinking.
